5 — Integration points
External systems the platform connects to.
Swim Central
Read / webhook sync
National registration records, member IDs, club affiliations. Platform subscribes — does not write back.
Competition Upload
State CSV → Swim Central → national automation
Competition results via Swim Central submissions. Short-term: CSV import from state associations. Long-term: automated ingestion as results are submitted nationally.
HP / Assessment systems
Subscription + access gate
Biomechanics, race analysis, technique assessments. Gated by subscription tier — lower tiers see prompt only.
Online Payments
Card linked to account
Consumer subscription ($x/mo) and club fee collection. Card saved once to account — payments and fee transactions happen seamlessly without re-entering details.
AusSwim Database / Accreditation
Directory lookup + webhook
Instructor member numbers, accreditation levels, WWCC status. Club adds instructor by member number; platform verifies.
